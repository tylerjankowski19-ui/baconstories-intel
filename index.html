Platform Drama / Urgency Hooks
Roblox players have anxiety about missing updates, bans, or changes to their favorite games. ALL CAPS titles trigger the "must watch NOW" reflex. Works best when tied to a real event — an actual update, a moderation controversy, or a game going down.

PATTERN #5 · TIER A
Big Number / Reward Hook
Viewers stay for the full video if they think they might qualify for something. Even without a real giveaway, framing content around a large number — "100,000 Robux", "Top 1% of players", "only 3 people know this" — spikes completion rate.
