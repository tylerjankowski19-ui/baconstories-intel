✦ What's Working
Scenario Choice format is your highest-engagement content type. Viewers comment their choice every time, signaling strong interaction to the algorithm.
Family and school drama scenarios consistently outperform abstract topics in your niche. You are in the right lane — stay here.
The VIP unlock mechanic tied to like-and-subscribe converts passive viewers into active subscribers every time it's executed correctly.
▾ What Isn't Working
Title specificity is your biggest lever right now. Vague titles lose the scroll. "You Got Caught" gets ignored. "Your Teacher Called Your Parents During Class 💀" stops the scroll cold.
Inconsistent posting cadence reduces algorithm distribution. The Shorts algorithm rewards creators who post on a predictable schedule — aim for every 2 days minimum.
Weak hook openers. Starting with a generic setup loses viewers in the first 2 seconds. Every hook must open with HEY GUYS, GUYS, or WAIT to stop the scroll before the scenario begins.
◆ Tips To Improve
Add one specific noun to every title before posting. The formula: Who + What Happened + Reaction Emoji. "Your Little Brother Broke The TV And Blamed You 💀😭" is the template.
Batch record 3 Shorts in one session and schedule them 2 days apart. Consistency beats burst-posting for Shorts growth every time.
Your CTA needs a direct question. "Comment below" gets ignored. "Comment A, B, C, or VIP — which one are you picking?" gives viewers one easy thing to do and floods the comment section.
Study your highest-performing video title structure and replicate it for the next 3 uploads. Don't change the formula — repeat the pattern that already worked.
